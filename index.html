Welcome José Andrés to Avaya ENGAGE 2023!

“Cooking is the beginning of everything I understand as happiness.”

Welcome Mark Pollock to Avaya ENGAGE 2023!

Explorer. Athlete. Motivational Speaker. Mark Pollock’s story is about building resilience and being part of a team. Unbroken by blindness in 1998, Mark became an adventure athlete competing in ultra-endurance races across all terrain, becoming the first blind person to race to the South Pole.

In 2010, a fall from a second story window nearly killed him. Mark broke his back and the damage to his spinal cord left him paralyzed. He’s been the subject of the acclaimed documentaries, “Blind Man Walking” and “Unbreakable – The Mark Pollock Story,” while authoring his own story, “Making It Happen.”

Hear Mark speak during the Monday, June 19 General Session.

Mark inspires leaders and their teams to build resilience and achieve more than they thought possible.

Welcome Katie Linendoll to Avaya ENGAGE 2023!

Technology expert. Award-winning TV personality. Katie Linendoll joins us as the Avaya ENGAGE master of ceremonies.

Katie has contributed hundreds of written pieces for top outlets and can regularly be seen on TV and digital segments such as the TODAY Show, Rachael Ray, The Weather Channel and CBS Sports Radio, just to name a few. 

She’s been named a Silicon Valley Visionary and leads an active lifestyle while also devoting a large percentage of her time to select philanthropic causes in healthcare, retired K9s, and US veterans. She’s also a country music artist whose songs and videos have hit the charts.

Join Katie as she hosts each of our General Sessions from the main stage.

One of the leading women in the field of technology, hosting numerous TV series and special events.

Welcome Dr. Rana el Kaliouby to Avaya ENGAGE 2023!

Egyptian-American scientist, entrepreneur, investor and an AI thought leader. Dr. Rana el Kaliouby is an on a mission to humanize technology before it dehumanizes us.

She is the Deputy CEO at Smart Eye and formerly, Co-Founder and CEO of Affectiva, an MIT spin-out. Rana is also General Partner at AI Operators Fund, an early-stage AI-focused venture fund, as well as an executive fellow at the Harvard Business School where she teaches about AI and startups.

Rana’s track record includes translating technology innovations into products that address massive market needs including in automotive, health and robotics.

Hear Rana speak during the Wednesday, June 21 General Session.

Author of Girl Decoded: A Scientist’s Quest to Reclaim Our Humanity by Bringing Emotional Intelligence to Technology
